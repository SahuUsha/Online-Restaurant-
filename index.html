About our Resturant

Resturant Name

Users search for and identify places to eat or order from Resturant names

  • # Restaurant names on OlineMeal must be written as they appears on the board outside the resturant.
  • # Restaurant establishment types and taglines(unless the resturant name is registered with the tagline)must not be mentioned in the name of the resturant on OnlineMeal.
  • # Restaurant abbreviations in the resturant names is not accepted on OnlineMeal

Restaurant Address

A dinner looks for these facilities when deciding about where to dine. These are called attribute tags on OnlineMeal.

  • #The Pure Veg(no meat and egg)tag is used for restaurants that serve only vegetarian food
  • # Happy Hours are exclusively for restaurants that serve non-alcholoic drink and that offer special offers or discounted rates during a period in the day
  • # Wi-Fi Available is marked if the dinners can use Wi-Fi services at the restaurant and not only by the management.

Business Hours

The operational hours of a restaurantn is represented as business hours so that others can plan their convenient time.

Users can filter restaurants based on a restaurant opening hours by using the"Open at" filter on OnlineMeal.
  • # A 24-hours clock is used to add timing to a listing. This is displayed on the restaurant page in a 12-hour clock format.
  • # We always add the timing for the dine-in hours. If the delivery hours are from the dine-in timings.we can add the custom delivery timing to your page if you mail us at restaurant@OnlineMail.com.
  • # The timing grids on OnlineMeal accept only numerals,so only specific times can be entered. Entering specific timing as specified above will help the users find your restaurant using the filter'Open at'if they search for a specific time while choosing a restaurant.
